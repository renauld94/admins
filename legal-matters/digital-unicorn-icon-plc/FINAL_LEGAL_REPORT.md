### Appendix C: Legal Doctrines Reference

**1. Promissory Estoppel**
- Promise made by authorized party
- Intended to induce reliance
- Actual reasonable reliance
- Detrimental reliance
- Injustice can only be avoided by enforcement

**2. Implied Covenant of Good Faith and Fair Dealing**
- Every contract contains implied duty
- Parties must not frustrate contract purposes
- Cannot prevent other party from receiving contract benefits
- Bad faith conduct may void termination or create additional damages

**3. Breach of Contract**
- Valid contract
- Performance by claimant
- Breach by defendant
- Damages resulting from breach

**4. Defamation**
- False statement of fact
- Publication to third parties
- Harm to reputation
- Malice or negligence (depending on jurisdiction)

**5. Wrongful Termination**
- Termination without cause (if contract requires cause)
- Termination without proper procedure
- Retaliation for protected activity
- Bad faith termination
