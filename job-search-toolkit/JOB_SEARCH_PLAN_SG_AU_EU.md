# Job Search Playbook — Singapore, Australia, Europe (Nov 2025)

Audience: Simon Renauld — Data Engineer & QA Automation Specialist (HCMC, GMT+7)
Goal: Secure a role in 6–8 weeks in SG/AU/EU aligned to QA/QC Manager, Data Quality Lead, or Data/Analytics Engineer with strong QA.

## Target roles

- QA & QC Manager (Data Platforms)
- Data Quality Lead / Analytics QA Lead
- Data Engineer / Analytics Engineer (with test automation focus)
- Data SRE / Platform QA (Airflow, CI/CD, observability)

## Market approach by region

### Singapore (SG)

- Work model: Onsite/hybrid preferred; remote-friendly possible with SG entities operating regionally.
- Visa: Employment Pass (EP). Many companies consider EP for niche profiles; emphasize immediate availability and regional presence (HCMC, 2h flight).
- Boards: LinkedIn, JobStreet, e27, MyCareersFuture (check EP eligibility note), company careers.
- Companies to prioritize: Shopee/Sea, Grab, Lazada, GovTech, Singtel, DBS/OCBC/UOB, Gojek (SG presence), PayPal, ByteDance/TikTok, Agoda.

### Australia (AU)

- Work model: Mostly onsite/hybrid; remote if contractor. Time zone close enough for collaboration.
- Visa: Skilled migration is slower; target employer-sponsored roles and remote-first contracts. Use SEEK, LinkedIn, EthicalJobs (if relevant), company careers.
- Companies: Atlassian, Canva, Xero, WiseTech Global, REA Group, SEEK, Afterpay/Block, CSIRO spinouts, banks (CBA, ANZ, Westpac, NAB).

### Europe (EU/UK)

- Work model: Remote-first or relocation (Germany, NL, FR, UK). Highlight GDPR/compliance experience.
- Visa: Prioritize remote-first companies or those with relocation programs; UK Skilled Worker if sponsor-listed.
- Boards: LinkedIn, EU Tech Jobs, Otta (UK/EU), Welcome to the Jungle (FR), Indeed, company careers.

## Positioning (profile messaging)

- Headline: "Data Engineer & QA Automation — 99.9%+ data accuracy, CI/CD for data, Airflow + pytest"
- Summary bullets:
  - Built automated test pipelines (Airflow, pytest) across multi-source data (e‑commerce, healthcare).
  - Quality-first approach: prevent defects, measurable SLAs (freshness, accuracy, coverage).
  - Scaled QA to 500M+ records; embedded with dev teams; ship fast with guardrails.
- Region note: "Open to remote or relocation (SG/AU/EU). Based in Ho Chi Minh City (GMT+7)."

## 4-week plan

- Week 1: Assets ready (CV x2, LinkedIn refresh, templates), queries + alerts, 30 target companies/region, warm intros list.
- Week 2: 40 applications (tailored), 10 warm intros, 2 tech screens scheduled.
- Week 3: 40 applications, 10 targeted reach-outs, complete 1–2 take-home demos (QA pipeline mini-project).
- Week 4: Focus on interview loops; continue 20 high-fit apps; negotiate.

## Daily 30–45 min routine

1) Run quick searches (SG, AU, EU queries) and capture 5 high-fit leads into CRM.
2) Send 2–3 tailored applications (CV v1: QA/Quality Lead; CV v2: Data/Analytics Engineer).
3) 3 reach-outs: recruiter + hiring manager + engineer (short DM).
4) Update CRM; set follow-ups.

## Success criteria

- 10–12 applications/week with fit ≥8/10.
- 3 recruiter calls and 1–2 technical screens/week by Week 2.
- 1 offer by Week 6–8.

## Edge considerations

- Visa: Be explicit about relocation/remote readiness; mention EP (SG), sponsorship (AU/UK), and EU remote.
- Salary bands: SG (S$7–12k/mo), AU (A$130–180k base), EU (EUR 65–100k), UK (£65–95k) depending on level and role.
- Time zones: Prefer GMT+3 to GMT+10 compatible teams for remote.

## How to use this repo

- Use `job-search/search_queries_sg_au_eu.md` for copy/paste searches on each board.
- Add companies to `job-search/target_companies_*.json`; track progress in your PostgreSQL CRM.
- Use `job-search/outreach_templates_fr_en.md` to message recruiters/hiring managers.
- Optionally run `linkedin-automation/quick_daily_search.sh` and your stealth browser to assist manual research.
