### 7. Human Voice vs AI Detection

Made conscious effort to sound human:

**AI-sounding:** "Spearheaded the implementation of cutting-edge ML infrastructure"  
**Human:** "I've spent my career turning complex data challenges into practical solutions"

**AI-sounding:** "Collaborated with cross-functional stakeholders to drive synergies"  
**Human:** "I managed a diverse group of 50+ people, helping them do their best work"

**Techniques:**
- Used contractions (I've, don't, it's)
- Varied sentence length (short punchy ones, longer descriptive ones)
- Included personality ("no small feat," "at 2 AM," "ready to build something meaningful")
- Avoided buzzwords (synergy, leveraged, spearheaded, thought leadership)
- Used active voice and first person
