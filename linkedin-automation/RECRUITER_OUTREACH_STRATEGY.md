## üöÄ **PRO TIPS**

### ‚úÖ DO:
- **Connect on Mobile**: LinkedIn allows longer connection notes on mobile app (up to 300 chars)
- **Personalize Every Message**: Mention their company or a specific role they posted
- **Be Specific**: "Data Engineer in Singapore" not "any job"
- **Follow Up**: If they accept, send thank you message within 24 hours
- **Engage with Their Posts**: Like/comment on their job postings to stay visible

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Don't spam: 3 connections/day max (LinkedIn limit: ~100/week)
- Don't send generic "I'd like to add you" (no note)
- Don't mention "desperate" or "urgent"
- Don't send resume in first message (offer it in follow-up)
- Don't connect with 10 recruiters from same agency (pick 1-2 best)
